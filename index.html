Rasoi Live Concept
Version 2.0

An app which brings you food in just three steps.

FEATURES

Offline Ordering

No Internet should not be a reason for you to stay Hungry, So we are introducing "No Internet Ordering"

Reorder from History

Most of the people likes to order the same food, so we believe we should be giving you the same in a click.

Live Tracking on Map

As foodies we know what it's like to wait for food and constantly asking where your food is, so now you know what we do.

Live tracking on map

Once you have ordered your food, its hard to wait for it come. You constantly want to know where it has reached. RasoiLive enables live tracking of your order. Now, there is no need to call again and again to ask where your food has reached and how long will it take to reach you. Tracking the food is now one click away. Just tap the track button and get liv tracking of your order.

Reorder from history

Generally, our taste buds crave for the same taste again and again. Now, ordering that favourite food is just one click away and when you are a crazy lazy foodie, you must not want to peep into the large menu for the same food you always order. So, without spending much of your valuable time, go to the history and reorder the same food .
